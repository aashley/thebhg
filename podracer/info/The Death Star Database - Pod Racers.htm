Pod Racers

Aldar Beedo

Aldar's pod is blue and yellow and can be recognized by it's box-shaped engines and elongated cockpit. Aldar Beedo is a Glymphid hitman who often works for fellow racers to put them out of action. When Wan Sandage hired Beedo to assassinate Sebulba, Beedo entered the Boonta Eve Classic. He was unable to kill Sebulba in the race. Beedo's vehicle relies on sheer power and it's aerodynamic design to win racers. It is especially good on straighaways, but it has trouble pulling corners.

Manufacturer: Manta RamAir
Cockpit Lenght: 5.28 meters
Engine Length: 10.59 meters
Top Speed: 823 kph
Special Modifications: Aerodynamic Cockpit

Anakin Skywalker

Created from scrap supplies found in Watto's Junkyard, Anakin Skywalker built a podracer that proved to be the fastest pod in the Boonta Eve Classic. The pod is uses a Ulzer 620C engine. The racer's air scoops in front of each engine increase the vehicle's maneuverability, while multiple air brakes allow Anakin to make sudden stops and corner effectively. A fuel atomiser and distribution system further increase the racer's performance.

Manufacturer: Radon-Ulzer & Anakin Skywalker
Cockpit Lenght: 3.15 meters
Engine Length: 6.5 meters
Top Speed: 947 kph
Special Modifications: Air Brakes and innovative fuel atomiser

Ark Roose

Ark uses a bizarre, low performance racer with a compact Plug-8G engine in a small cockpit. The racer is notable for its squat twin-turbine engines which provide moderate thrust and are very maneuverable. Roose has raced in numerous events and is aware of Anakin's skill. Roose planned on sabatoging Anakin's racer before the event but accidentaly sabatoged Ben's pod instead.

Manufacturer: Vokoff-Strood
Cockpit Lenght: 1.83 meters
Engine Length: 4.5 meters
Top Speed: 775 kph
Special Modifications: Twin Turbine Engines

Ben Quadinaro

Ben rides in a unique 'quadra-pod' with four smaller engines connected to one cockpit. Being so large, Ben was not able to get a racer and was forced to rent one before the Boonta Eve Classic after recieving a challenge from Boles Roor. Being a frightened Toong, Ben has never finished an event as dangerous as the Boonta Eve Race. Before the race, Ark Roose tampered with the engines of his pod which stalled the his takeoff. Later, his power-couplink failed, which sent the four engines in all directions.

Manufacturer: Balta-Trabaat
Cockpit Lenght: 2.72 meters
Engine Length: 4.5 meters
Top Speed: 940 kph
Special Modifications: none

Boles Roor

Boles Roor's podracer is known for it's massive sphercial engines and exceedingly long cockpit. Boles himself is known as a notorious drunkard and he earns a fortune as a famous glimmik singer. He frequently gambes on podraces, however, much of his wealth is lost as he bets on the wrong ships. Boles is also not known to be a talented pilot himself. His podracer is unmodified and is known for it's poor welding and sub-standard components.

Manufacturer: Bin Gassi
Cockpit Lenght: 6.86 meters
Engine Length: 7.39 meters
Top Speed: 790 kph
Special Modifications: Dual combustion chambers

Clegg Holdfast

Clegg is a journalist for Podracer Quarterly. His racer is one of the largest in the Boonta Eve Classic, as his Keizer-Volvec uses massive and powerful engines. While his Wasp is well built, Clegg is a poor racer. If driven by a better pilot, tehe Wasp could prove to be a worthy opponent.

Manufacturer: Keizer-Volvec
Cockpit Lenght:4.42 meters
Engine Length: 10.36 meters
Top Speed: 800 kph
Special Features: Protective Canopy

Dud Bolt

Dud Bolt is one of Sebulba's henchmen who keeps the track clear for him during events. With the help of his durable podracer, Dud Bolt whill do anything to collide into other racers, as he recieves a bonus for each one he disables. Bolt flies in a durable pod which can be identified by it's prolonged engines.

Manufacturer: Vulptereen
Cockpit Lenght: 3.66 meters
Engine Length: 7.92 meters
Top Speed: 760 kph
Special Modifications: Durasteel-reinforced frame

Ebe Endocott

Ebe's racer has long, pointed engines. His J930 has a variation of the Split-X Configuration like Sebulba. A vetran of several Malastare Podraces, Ebe was able to purchase a Corellian Freighter with his winnings. He used the ship to travel to Tatooine were he hopped to win the Boona Eve Classic. Ebe is a confident and talented racer, and does not feel threatened by Sebulba.

Manufacturer: JAK Racing
Cockpit Lenght: 3.05 meters
Engine Length: 9.55 meters
Top Speed: 785 kph
Special Features: Split-X configured racing engines

Elan Mak

Elan Mak, whose real name is Kan Nale, was the son of a Fluggrian crime lord who was murdered by Aldar Beedo. Planning for revenge, Nale entered in the Boonta Eve Classic under the name of Elan Mak; the opposite of his real name. Nale's podracer has a round cockpit with buldous engines. The pod proves to be very effective and durable. Neural bundles in Nale's forhead allow him to process sensory input at very high speeds, which greatly increases his reaction time.

Manufacturer: Kurtob
Cockpit Lenght: 2.03 meters
Engine Length: 3.81 meters
Top Speed: 775 kph
Special Features: Round Cockpit

Gasgano

This racer is custom built for the twenty-four fingered Ord Perdrovia, Gasgano. The racer has stabilizing fins on the two massive racer engines, which help provide extra stability in slow and shallow corners of a race track. Gasgano is sponsored by Gardulla the Hutt. He is known for his amazing reflexing and his ability to perform multiple tasks simultaneously.

Manufacturer: Ord Pedrovia
Cockpit Lenght: 2.72 meters
Engine Length: 6.71 meters
Top Speed: 823 kph
Special Features: Dual front stabilizing fins

Mars Pondrat

Mars was infatuated by Ann Gella, who was one of Sebulba's slaves. After Sebulba discovered this he vowed that Mars would never finish the race.

Manufacturer: Collor Pondrat

Special Features: Reinforced rudder-skids

Neva Kee

Neva Kee is one of the rare podracers who puts her podrace engines behind the cockpit, which is technically an illegan act. In addition, she also uses durasteel arms to connect the cockpit to the engine, rather than the conventional steelton cables. With the cockpit in front of the engines, Neva Kee is given a clear view of the terrian, however, she will take the brunt of a collision, or if the engine explodes it would cause severe injuries.

Manufacturer: Farwan & Glott
Cockpit Lenght: 2.78 meters
Engine Length: 7.16 meters
Top Speed: 785kph
Special Features: Forward Mounted Cockpit attatched wth durasteel arms

Ratts Tyrell

Ratts Tyrell flies in a typical podracer with two elongated engines and massive turbine intakes. In the Boonta Eve Classic, Ratts proved to be a worthy competitor. However, while Ratts had enhanced metabolism and incredible reflexes, he suffered a devastating collision in a cave during the race. Ratts Tyrell was disintigrated with his racer.

Manufacturer: Vokoff-Strood
Cockpit Lenght: 2.03 meters
Engine Length: 9.63 meters
Top Speed: 841 kph
Special Modifications: Enlarged turbine intakes

Sebulba

Sebulba pilots a heavily modified and highy illegal podracer. Extremely durable, Sebulba is allowed to collide with other racers during an event and knock them off of cliffs, into walls and other haszards. In extreme circumstances, Sebulba resorts to an illegal flame thrower mounted in the racer's engine. While the podracer was nearly distroyed during the Boonta Eve Classic, Sebulba managed to survive and will undoubtedly return to his sport.

Manufacturer: Collor Pondrat
Cockpit Lenght: 3.96 meters
Engine Length: 7.8 meters
Top Speed: 829 kph
Special Modifications: Illegal flame emitter on right side of racer

Teemto Pagalies

Teemto's podracer has a circular cockpit which makes it unique. The cockpit was built around a gyroscopic stabilizer and was supported by a rudder. Becamse the racer lacked a canopy, Teemto relied on numerous sensor readouts and a monitor to watch to track. During the Boonta Eve Classic, Teemto was the racer with the longest engines. During the race, however, Teemto's racer was shot by Tusken Raiders which was destroyed in the explosion. It is not confirmed whether or not the Mandellian survived the crash, but his circular cockpit most likely saved him from death.

Manufacturer: Irdani Performance Group
Cockpit Lenght: 5.33 meters
Engine Length: 10.67 meters
Top Speed: 775 kph
Special Modifications: Circular cockpit with gyrostabiliser

Wan Sandage

Wan Sandage is a playboy and a podracing expert. His Elsinore-Cordova has a distinctive large fin and an insectoid appearance. At age six and already a rapidy aging Devlikk, Sandage entered the Boonta Eve Classic in hope of finally achieving victory in a race. Realizing Sebulba would probably win the race, Sandage hired hitman/racer Aldar Beedo, who failed to kill the dug before the race.

Manufacturer: Elsinor-Cordova
Cockpit Lenght: 2.06 meters
Engine Length: 5.03 meters
Top Speed: 785 kph
Special Features: Cockpit sail
